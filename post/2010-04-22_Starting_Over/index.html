Starting Over

2010-04-22 2 min read # 杂谈

I'm starting over, after the wave of Toefl has passed, and the failure of
several attempts of my research. Everything ended at 9 p.m today, and starts
over towards a promising future, at least I hopefully think so.

CXX left today. She is nice. She called us to the lab and told us everything I
needed to continue the research during her days in Changsha, and she gave me
the key of our
warehouse

From tomorrow on, we will test our sample by western blotting everyday until
there is a ideal result. CXX sets us a mission of three protein before she
comes back, and said this would be a remarkable achievement if we could do it.
Well, I believe that we can, and I'll try.

Na is facing her Toefl this Saturday, I know it's not easy for her, especially
due to some mental reasons, anyway I hope she doesn't have too much burden and
just bring it on, everything will be
fine.
